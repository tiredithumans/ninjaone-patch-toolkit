# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1** as its code of
conduct. The full text is published at:

- https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

In short: we are committed to a harassment-free, welcoming, and respectful
experience for everyone who participates in this project, regardless of
background or identity. Be kind, assume good faith, and keep discussion focused
on the work.

## Scope

This applies within all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions, and
any other project communication channels) and when an individual is officially
representing the project in public spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately to
the project maintainer, [@tiredithumans](https://github.com/tiredithumans), via the
contact details on that GitHub profile.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers
are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may
take appropriate, fair corrective action — including editing or removing
contributions, or temporarily or permanently banning a contributor — in response
to behavior they deem inappropriate. The enforcement guidelines in the
Contributor Covenant 2.1 (Correction → Warning → Temporary Ban → Permanent Ban)
apply.
